Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Giveaway Go ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application ("app").

What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our app:

  • Personal information, such as your name and email address, which you provide to us when you participate in a giveaway contest.
  • Device information, such as your device type and operating system, which we automatically collect when you use our app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:
  • Personalize your experience in the app, for example, by displaying relevant giveaways and recommendations.
  • Send you notifications about new giveaway contests.
  • Track usage statistics to improve the app's performance, for example, by identifying and fixing bugs.

We use your device information to:

  • Ensure that the app is compatible with your device.
  • Analyze how users interact with the app to improve its performance.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • We use Firebase Messaging plugin to send notifications to your device. Please note that we are not responsible for the data handling practices of Firebase Messaging.
  • We use Ad Mob plugin to display advertisements in the app. Please note that we are not responsible for the data handling practices of Ad Mob.
Your data is protected using encryption technologies and is safely stored in our database. You can access and modify your data using web tokens for authentication.

How You Can Control Your Information

You can edit your personal information, such as your name, email address, and password, up to 3 times per day in the app. If you request to delete your personal information from our database, it will be permanently deleted within 1 day after final confirmation through email.

Age Restriction

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), which requires us to obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our app, please contact us at contact@arenabear.com.
